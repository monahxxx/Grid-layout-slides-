Good to know

  • A grid container establishes a new grid formatting context for its contents
  • Margins of adjacent grid items do not collapse
  • Avoid using percentages in paddings or margins on grid items entirely
  • Grid indexes are writing mode relative. F.e. in Arabic language the first column is the rightmost column
